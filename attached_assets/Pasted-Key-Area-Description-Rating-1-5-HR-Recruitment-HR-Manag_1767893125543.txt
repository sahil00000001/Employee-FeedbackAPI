Key Area	Description	Rating (1-5)	HR Recruitment & HR Manager Specific Considerations
Collaboration and Teamwork	How effectively do you collaborate with other team members, share information, and contribute to team success?		Collaborating with hiring managers, team leads, and other departments to understand staffing needs and align recruitment efforts.
Communication	How well do you communicate with peers, leaders, and stakeholders? This includes clarity, active listening, and feedback.		Effectively communicating with candidates, department heads, and leadership to ensure alignment on recruitment strategies and company needs.
Problem-Solving and Innovation	How effectively do you identify and solve challenges, propose improvements, and contribute innovative solutions?		Solving hiring challenges, improving recruitment processes, and suggesting new approaches to talent acquisition and HR management.
Work Quality and Attention to Detail	How consistently do you deliver high-quality work with attention to detail, meeting deadlines and standards?		Ensuring that job descriptions, candidate evaluations, and onboarding processes are meticulously crafted, accurate, and in line with organizational needs.
Accountability and Ownership	How well do you take ownership of your tasks, responsibilities, and outcomes, ensuring follow-through and delivery?		Taking full responsibility for recruitment and HR processes, ensuring timely hiring, smooth onboarding, and effective employee engagement.
Adaptability and Flexibility	How open are you to change and adapting to new tools, processes, or requirements in a fast-paced environment?		Adapting to changing hiring requirements, employee engagement needs, or evolving HR technologies while ensuring the organization’s HR policies remain up-to-date.
Knowledge Sharing and Training	How actively do you share your knowledge, help train others, and contribute to the team’s learning and growth?		Sharing HR best practices, recruitment strategies, and legal requirements with team members, and training others on recruitment tools and HR policies.
Mentorship and Support	How effectively do you support and mentor junior team members, providing guidance and assistance in their growth?		Mentoring junior HR team members or recruiters, guiding them through the recruitment process, candidate engagement, and professional development.
Personal Development (Training/Certification)	How committed are you to your professional development through continuous learning, certifications, and skill enhancement?		Pursuing certifications in HR, talent acquisition, employee relations, and keeping up with the latest recruitment technologies and trends.
Work/Task Completion	How effectively and efficiently do you complete assigned tasks and meet deadlines?		Managing end-to-end recruitment processes, meeting hiring deadlines, and ensuring smooth and timely onboarding for new hires.
Contribution to Team and Company Goals	How well do you align your work with team and company objectives, contributing to the overall success of the team and organization?		Aligning recruitment and HR efforts with organizational goals, helping the company achieve staffing and diversity objectives.
Participation in Recruitment and Team Building	How involved are you in recruitment, interviews, or team-building activities that contribute to strengthening the team?		Actively participating in interviews, recruitment drives, and contributing to team-building activities that foster a positive work culture.
